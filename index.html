Year One Overview

This year has been an interesting and challenging year for me. I originally chose to do this course as a way to do full time ministry. I saw that I had something to offer my local community and was finding it hard balancing University with my voluntary participation in youth work. I saw Praxis as an easy entry into helping out, whilst still partaking in some professional development to be able to acheive my goals more effectively.

The course has exceeded my expectations; professional development is certainly what I got. Praxis has impacted all aspects of my life because it demands that all aspects of my life are reflected upon. I can say without a doubt that I am a better leader and a different person at the end of year one to how I started. I am pleased that I was told at the start that "you get out what you put into the course". Unlike University, where the course is rigidly prescribed, with Praxis there is no limit to how you can reflect on your work and hopefully no limit on how well you can do your work with people.

This website looks at some of my journey this year, the roles I have taken part in, some personal reflections, how I have applied four aspects of mission theology to my youth practice and feedback from the people I have connected with over the year.
